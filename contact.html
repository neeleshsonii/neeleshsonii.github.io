Frequently Asked Questions

You can register for events by clicking the "Register" button on any event post. You'll be redirected to a registration form specific to that event. Make sure you're logged in to your CAMPUSQUEST account before registering.

Only authorized club representatives can post events. Contact the website administrators through this page to verify your club and get posting privileges. You'll need to provide your club details and official documentation.

If you're having login issues, try clearing your browser cache and cookies. Make sure you're using the correct phone number and name. If problems persist, contact our technical support team through the contact form above.
